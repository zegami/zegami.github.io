Privacy

What we collect, why we collect it, and how you can reach us about it.

Last updated April 2026

This page covers zegami.com

This notice describes data handling on the Zegami marketing website at zegami.com only. The Zegami application at app.zegami.com is a separate service with its own data-processing terms — when you sign in there, the terms presented during sign-in govern.

What we collect on zegami.com

We aim to collect as little as possible:

  • No analytics, no third-party trackers, no advertising cookies. If that changes we’ll list the processor and purpose here first.
  • No account sign-up on this site. Account creation happens in the app, not on the marketing pages.
  • Server logs retained by our static-hosting provider (GitHub Pages / GitHub Inc.) contain the usual IP address, user agent, and requested URL. We don’t read or export these.
  • Fonts are served by Google Fonts. Google Fonts receives your IP address as part of the font-file request. See Google’s privacy policy for their handling.

When you contact us

If you send an email via one of the mailto: links on this site (for example “Book a demo”), your message is delivered to our inbox and kept there for the duration of the conversation plus a reasonable archival period. We use that address to reply; we don’t add it to any mailing list without your explicit opt-in.

Third parties embedded on this page

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com) — serves the typefaces used on this site.
  • jsDelivr (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — serves the Swagger UI assets on /api-docs only. No third-party code runs on any other page.

Nothing else. No Facebook Pixel, no Hotjar, no Segment, no Intercom.

Your rights

If you’re in the UK, EU, or another jurisdiction with a right-to-access or right-to-erasure regime, contact us at hello@zegami.com and we’ll respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions or corrections: hello@zegami.com.

Visual exploration

Browse thousands of items as a visual grid. Sort, filter, and group by any field. Zoom from overview to individual record in a click.

Image collections

Purpose-built for datasets where images matter: camera traps, microscopy, aerial surveys, historical archives. See images alongside metadata, not buried in folders.

Geospatial

Plot your data on a map. Filter spatially. Understand how location relates to everything else in your dataset.

Time series & charts

Layer in temporal data. Compare trends across groups. Link chart selections back to individual records.

Filtering & faceting

Combine filters across any dimension — numeric ranges, categories, text search, date ranges. Every filter updates every view in real time.

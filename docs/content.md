## Problem / Why Section

### Heading
Your data is bigger than your tools

### Body
Spreadsheets flatten everything into rows. Dashboards show you summaries. Neither lets you actually look at your data.

Zegami gives you the full picture — literally. Lay out thousands of images, data points, or documents visually. Filter, sort, group, and zoom in on what matters. Spot patterns that no chart would show you.

## Core Capabilities Section

### Heading
One platform. Every data type.

### 1. Visual Exploration
Browse thousands of items as a visual grid. Sort, filter, and group by any field. Zoom from overview to individual record in a click.

[SCREENSHOT: grid view with colour-coded grouping]

### 2. Image Collections
Purpose-built for datasets where images matter — camera traps, microscopy, aerial surveys, historical archives. See the images alongside the metadata, not buried in folders.

[SCREENSHOT: image collection with metadata panel open]

### 3. Geospatial
Plot your data on a map. Filter spatially. Understand how location relates to everything else in your dataset.

[SCREENSHOT: map view with clustered points]

### 4. Time Series & Charts
Layer in temporal data. Compare trends across groups. Link chart selections back to individual records.

[SCREENSHOT: chart/time series view]

### 5. Filtering & Faceting
Combine filters across any dimension — numeric ranges, categories, text search, date ranges. Every filter updates every view in real time.

[SCREENSHOT: filter panel with multiple active filters]

## Use Cases Section

### Heading
Proven across sectors

### Life Sciences
High-throughput imaging, phenotyping, pathology, microscopy. Researchers use Zegami to visually QA and explore imaging datasets that are too large to review manually.

### Environmental Monitoring
Camera trap review, biodiversity surveys, habitat assessment. Explore species detections across sites, time periods, and sensor types in a single view.

### Digital Humanities
Museum collections, historical archives, manuscript analysis. Scholars use Zegami to explore and annotate large visual collections without specialist software.

### Industrial & Enterprise
Quality control imaging, asset inspection, document review. Any workflow where people need to visually assess large volumes of data.

## How It Works Section

### Heading
From data to insight in minutes

### 1. Connect your data
Upload a spreadsheet, connect to an API, or point Zegami at an image folder. Structured and unstructured data, together.

### 2. Explore visually
Every record becomes a tile. Filter, sort, and group across any combination of fields. Switch between grid, map, chart, and detail views.

### 3. Share and collaborate
Publish collections for your team or publicly. Save views. Export filtered subsets. No training required — if you can use a web browser, you can use Zegami.

## Differentiator Section

### Heading
Not a dashboard. Not a database.

### Body
Dashboards aggregate your data into charts. Databases let you query it. Zegami lets you *see* it — every item, every image, every data point — and navigate it visually.

Built for datasets with thousands to millions of records. Born from research at the University of Oxford. Used in production by research institutions, government agencies, and commercial organisations.
